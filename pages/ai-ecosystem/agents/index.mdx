# Memgraph Agents

**Memgraph Agents** are specialized tools designed to streamline and enhance the
development of graph applications. These agents leverage **Large Language Models
(LLMs)** to provide intelligent solutions for various graph-related tasks.

- [SQL2Graph Agent](/ai-ecosystem/agents/sql2graph-agent): AI-powered relational
  to graph migration.
- [Unstructured2Graph Agent](/ai-ecosystem/agents/unstructured2graph-agent):
  unstructured document ingestion and knowledge graph creation.
